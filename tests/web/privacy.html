Privacy Policy

Effective Date: December 20, 2024

At Carolina Lumper Service, we value your privacy and strive to protect your personal information. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your data.

Privacy Policy Details

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide to Us

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, and other identifiers.
  • Account Information: Login credentials, company details, and usage preferences.

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Usage Data: Information about interactions with the Software, including IP address, device type, and usage patterns.
  • Cookies: We use cookies to enhance your browsing experience.

1.3 Information from Third Parties

  • When you connect third-party services (e.g., QuickBooks), we may collect relevant account details and transactions.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  1. Provide and improve the Software and Website.

  4. Process payments and business operations.
  5. Comply with legal obligations.

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. However, we may share it with:

  • Service Providers: For hosting, analytics, or payment processing.

4. Data Retention

We retain your information as long as necessary for the purposes outlined or as required by law. Unnecessary data is securely deleted or anonymized.

5. Data Security

We use industry-standard measures to protect your data but cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  1. Access and update your information.
  2. Request data deletion.

  4. File complaints with regulatory authorities.

To exercise these rights, email us at info@carolinalumpers.com.
