About Carolina Lumper Service

Learn more about our mission, our values, and how we strive to provide the best freight handling solutions in the industry.

Who We Are

Carolina Lumper Service (CLS) was founded with the goal of providing efficient, reliable, and high-quality freight handling services. With over 15 years of experience in the industry, our team has built a reputation for precision, professionalism, and dedication.

We pride ourselves on our ability to deliver flexible and tailored solutions to meet the needs of warehouses and distribution centers, whether during peak periods or throughout everyday operations.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple: to provide seamless freight handling that allows our clients to focus on growing their business. We aim to make supply chains more efficient through our range of services—from unloading and sorting to custom project support—while maintaining the highest standards of accuracy and speed.

Our Values

Reliability

Our clients rely on us for timely, precise services, and we consistently deliver without compromise.

Integrity

We operate transparently and are committed to maintaining trust through honest communication and quality work.

Flexibility

Every client has unique needs, and our team is always ready to tailor our services to exceed expectations.

Meet Our Team

Our team is comprised of experienced professionals who understand the demands of warehouse operations and logistics. Our employees and contractors are trained to handle various aspects of freight logistics with precision, ensuring a seamless workflow for our clients.

From our reliable lumpers to our dedicated supervisors, we work together to ensure that every job is completed on time and to the highest quality standards.
