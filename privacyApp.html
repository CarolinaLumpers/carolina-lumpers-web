Employment Privacy Policy

Learn how Carolina Lumper Service protects your information during the application and employment process.

Our Commitment

Carolina Lumper Service (CLS) is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information during our recruitment and employment process.

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide through our application form, including:

  • Personal contact information (name, phone, email, address)
  • Work authorization status and documentation
  • Emergency contact information
  • Work preferences and availability
  • Experience and qualifications

How We Use Your Information

  • To evaluate your application and qualifications
  • To contact you about job opportunities
  • To verify work authorization (as required by law)
  • To communicate regarding scheduling and assignments

Information Protection

We protect your personal information through secure storage systems and limit access to authorized personnel only. We do not sell, share, or distribute your information to third parties except as required by law.

Your Rights

  • Request access to your personal information
  • Request corrections to inaccurate information
  • Withdraw your application at any time

Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy or your personal information, please contact us at:

jobs@carolinalumpers.com
